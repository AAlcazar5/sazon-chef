## üé¨ Animation Ideas

### Idle Animations
- Gentle breathing (subtle scale up/down)
- Slight head bob or hat tilt
- Blinking eyes
- Occasional smile or wink

### State-Specific Animations
- **Loading**: Stirring motion, or tapping foot patiently
- **Success**: Happy dance, thumbs up, confetti
- **Error**: Gentle head shake, reassuring smile
- **Empty State**: Looking around curiously, then offering help
- **Recipe Found**: Excited jump, clapping hands
- **Goal Achieved**: Celebration dance, confetti
- **Cooking**: Stirring, chopping motions

### Interactive Animations
- **On Tap**: Bounce or scale animation
- **On Swipe**: Follows finger, tilts in direction
- **On Scroll**: Peeks out from corner, follows scroll
- **On Notification**: Pops up, waves, points

## üé® Design Principles

### Visual Style
- **Rounded & Soft**: No sharp edges, everything is friendly
- **Warm Colors**: Primarily orange (brand), with warm accents
- **Expressive**: Large eyes, clear facial expressions
- **Simple**: Not overly detailed, works at small sizes
- **Scalable**: Looks good from icon size to full-screen

### Technical Considerations
- **Vector-based**: SVG for scalability
- **Layered**: Separate layers for animations (body, face, accessories)
- **Theme-aware**: Adapts to light/dark mode
- **Accessible**: High contrast, clear shapes
